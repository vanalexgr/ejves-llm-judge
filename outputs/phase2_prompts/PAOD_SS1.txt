Common Symptoms

Weak or Absent Pulse: A weak or absent pulse in the legs or feet can be detected by a healthcare provider.
Pain at Rest: In more severe cases, pain may occur in the legs and feet even at rest, especially when lying down (ischemic rest pain).
Less Common Symptoms

When to Seek Medical Help
